Modern Taurokathapsia

Taurokatapsia is the art of a person dominating over Nature, riding over it to prove the superiority of intelect over brute force. Metaheuristics, evolutionary computation is a bit like that: lets nature be your guide so that we can reach the optimum of a function we want to optimize. This was from the Knossos palace, part of the Minoan civilization, which is one of the oldest civilizations in Europe, contemporaneous to Egypt and to the old Assyrian empire. They had a written, and so far undeciphered, language, Linear A. One of the oldest civilizations, one of the oldest forms of sport and art. Let's see if we can learn something from them for our research.
